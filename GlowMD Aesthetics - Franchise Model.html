Benchmarks vs. Public Comparables

How this med-spa franchisor model compares to publicly traded franchise businesses (FY2025). Med-spa franchisors are mostly private, so these asset-light, royalty-driven franchisors are the closest public proxies — European Wax Center (beauty franchising) is the nearest structural match. Franchisor margins are high because revenue is royalties + fees, not unit operations. The GlowMD (this model) row updates live. "Rule of 40" = revenue growth % + EBITDA margin %.
